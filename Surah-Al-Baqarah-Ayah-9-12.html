Surah Al Baqarah: Ayah 9-12

By: Nouman Ali Khan

يُخَـٰدِعُونَ ٱللَّهَ وَٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ وَمَا يَخْدَعُونَ إِلَّآ أَنفُسَهُمْ وَمَا يَشْعُرُونَ

  • Hypocrisy is a big disease that can destroy guidance
  • We can never judge someone as a hypocrite
  • Hypocrisy is in the heart and only Allah knows what is in the Heart
  • There are two kinds of hypocrites
    1. Those who know he is a hypocrite
    2. Those who do not know who is a hypocrite
  • No one feels safe from hypocrisy except a hypocrite
  • No one fears hypocrisy except a believer
  • The symptoms of hypocrisy will always be there
  • Usamah bin Zaid was in battle with the disbeliever
  • The disbeliever dropped his sword then he accepted Islam
  • Usamah killed him anyway
  • The Prophet heard this and was angered by it
  • The Prophet said did you open his heart and see if he was a hypocrite
  • The Prophet told Usamah what will do when la illa ha ila Allah comes after you
  • There was an assignment attempt then next morning they blended with muslims
  • Allah revealed to the Prophet the names of the hypocrite
  • The Prophet told Hudaifa the names of these people
  • Umar asked Hudaifa is my name in it
  • Hudaifa said I can’t tell you but I know your name is not in the list
  • They are trying to deceive Allah and those who believe
  • The question arises how can anyone deceive Allah
  • The hypocrites are actually trying to deceive the Messenger and the believers
  • Allah is saying deceiving the Messenger is the same as deceiving Allah
  • They are not going to be deceiving anyone except themselves
  • They have no realization at all

فِى قُلُوبِهِم مَّرَضٌۭ فَزَادَهُمُ ٱللَّهُ مَرَضًۭا ۖ وَلَهُمْ عَذَابٌ أَلِيمٌۢ بِمَا كَانُوا۟ يَكْذِبُونَ

  • There is a disease in their hearts
  • Then Allah increased them in their disease
  • The disease is hypocrisy
  • Hypocrisy happens when you are trying to save yourself from other than Allah
  • Hypocrisy is trying to gain reward from other than the reward of Allah
  • Hypocrites are also trying to protect themselves
  • Hypocrites are like going through a lizards hole
  • They keep two doors open for their own protection
  • There is a good protecting of oneself through taqwa
  • There is a bad protecting of oneself through hypocrisy
  • Hypocrites have loyalties on both sides the believers and the disbelievers
  • And for these people especially they have a constantly painful punishment
  • Because of the lies that continuously make to themselves and Allah’s Messenger

وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمْ لَا تُفْسِدُوا۟ فِى ٱلْأَرْضِ قَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّمَا نَحْنُ مُصْلِحُونَ

  • And when it is said to them do not cause corruption in the earth
  • Whatever you do that is not genuine
  • Even good deeds that is not genuine is still corrupt
  • The corruption is that they plan with the disbelievers
  • They reply to the believers we are only peacemakers
  • They cover their cowardice with the guard of peace
  • Even though Allah and Messenger cut ties with them
  • This is another lie the hypocrites make
  • Hypocrites have no shame in declaring their self-righteousness

أَلَآ إِنَّهُمْ هُمُ ٱلْمُفْسِدُونَ وَلَـٰكِن لَّا يَشْعُرُونَ

  • Listen up! It is they who are the cause of corruption
  • They have no realization at all
