Surah Al Baqarah: Ayah 4-8

By: Nouman Ali Khan

وَٱلَّذِينَ يُؤْمِنُونَ بِمَآ أُنزِلَ إِلَيْكَ وَمَآ أُنزِلَ مِن قَبْلِكَ وَبِٱلْـَٔاخِرَةِ هُمْ يُوقِنُونَ

  • And those who believe and have the potential of believing
  • In that which was revealed to you O Prophet
  • And that which was revealed much before you
  • There are three main areas of faith which are belief in
    • Allah
    • The Message
      • The angels
      • The books
      • The Messenger
    • The Afterlife
  • Surah Baqarah focuses on believe in the message
  • This book is a privilege that we have been gifted with
  • There is no mention of any revelation after only this Quran and from before
  • People of taqwa only have believe in the Quran and scriptures of before
  • These people have conviction in the afterlife as if they experienced and seen it
  • The type of belief that can never be shaken
  • The real danger of the unseen is that of the afterlife

أُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ عَلَىٰ هُدًۭى مِّن رَّبِّهِمْ ۖ وَأُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ هُمُ ٱلْمُفْلِحُونَ

  • Those are the only ones who are committed to guidance from their Master
  • These people are far because they reached a very high level
  • These people are made as an example for others to follow
  • Those are the real ones who are going to succeed
  • There are two permanent attributes that have been given to the people of guidance
    • Mutaqeen – those who protect themselves
    • Muflioon – those who are successful

إِنَّ ٱلَّذِينَ كَفَرُوا۟ سَوَآءٌ عَلَيْهِمْ ءَأَنذَرْتَهُمْ أَمْ لَمْ تُنذِرْهُمْ لَا يُؤْمِنُونَ

  • No doubt those who disbelief and have the potential of disbelief
  • Even those that have a shred of doubt have no faith in their heart
  • This Quran removes all doubt
  • These people buried the seed of truth deep within themselves
  • It is the same for them whether you warn them or do not warn them
  • They do not believe and they will never believe
  • They were given the best of all speeches, the Quran
  • The best of all speakers, the Messenger
  • In the best of all ways and yet they still disbelieve

خَتَمَ ٱللَّهُ عَلَىٰ قُلُوبِهِمْ وَعَلَىٰ سَمْعِهِمْ ۖ وَعَلَىٰٓ أَبْصَـٰرِهِمْ غِشَـٰوَةٌۭ ۖ وَلَهُمْ عَذَابٌ عَظِيمٌۭ

  • Allah has completely sealed their hearts and their hearing
  • Allah sealed their hearts first
  • This surah mentions matters of the heart
  • Taqwa is mentioned and taqwa is in the heart
  • Faith is mentioned and faith is in the heart
  • Doubt is mentioned and doubt lies in the heart
  • And even on their eyes there is a covering
  • When someone’s heart is sealed they are no longer interested in the truth
  • When their hearing is sealed whatever you say has no impact on them
  • When there is a covering over their eyes
  • Then the character of the Prophet does not change them
  • And for these people exclusively there is great punishment

وَمِنَ ٱلنَّاسِ مَن يَقُولُ ءَامَنَّا بِٱللَّهِ وَبِٱلْيَوْمِ ٱلْـَٔاخِرِ وَمَا هُم بِمُؤْمِنِينَ

  • The three kinds of people are mentioned
    • Those who believe and have the potential of believing
    • Those who disbelief and have the potential of disbelieving
    • The hypocrites or those who say they believe but they don’t believe
  • Surah Fatihah mentions Alameen
  • And alameen are these three groups of people
  • And from amongst the people someone says we have believed in Allah
  • And we have even believed in the last day
  • This is the guilty conscience of the hypocrite speaking
  • Allah calls them that No they are not believers at all
