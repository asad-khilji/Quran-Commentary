By: Nouman Ali Khan

Introduction

  • The Quran is an incredible depiction of balance
  • This beautiful surah is balanced in a way that is humanly impossible

Praise and Thanks

  • If you just have praise then thanks is missing
  • If you just have thanks then praise is missing
  • Hamd includes both praise and thanks
  • Hamd is balanced

Ar Rahman Ar Raheem Malik Yom Ad Deen

  • If you just say Ar Rahman and Ar Raheem
  • Then people will take advantage of the Rahma of Allah
  • Hamd includes both praise and thanks
  • Malik Yom Ad Deen balances it

The Three Parts of the Fatihah

  • The first three ayat of the Fatihah is about Allah
  • The first half of the middle ayah is about Allah
  • The second half of the middle ayah is about us
  • The last three ayat of the Fatihah is about us

Nouns and Verbs

  • The first three ayat of the Fatihah is about Allah and it is noun based
  • Nouns are timeless and independent and Allah is timeless and independent
  • The first half of the middle ayah is noun based and it is about Allah
  • The second half of the middle ayah is verb based and it is about us
  • Verbs are temporary and dependent and we are temporary and dependent
  • The last three ayat of the Fatihah are verb based and it is about us

Balance in the Fatihah

  • The first three ayat of the Fatihah is about Allah
  • The first half of the middle ayah concludes it
  • The last three ayah of the Fatihah is about us
  • The second half of the middle ayah introduces it

Knowledge and Action

  • The first three ayat of the Fatihah is about knowledge
  • The middle ayah is about action
  • The last three ayat of the Fatihah is about acting upon knowledge and not acting on it

Alameen

  • Allah mentions in the first ayah that He is Master of all nations and people
  • All nations and people are summed up into three groups
    • Those who had knowledge and acted upon it and those whom Allah favored
    • Those who had knowledge but did not act on it and those who had wrath
    • Those who had no knowledge and acted wrongly and those who are lost
