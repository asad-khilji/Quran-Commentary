Surah Al Fatihah: Hadith Qudsi

Hadith Qudsi

Allah says, “I have divided the prayer between Me and My Slave, half of it is for Me and half of it is for My Slave.”

When we say, ٱلْحَمْدُ لِلَّهِ رَبِّ ٱلْعَـٰلَمِينَ

Allah says, “My Slave has Praised Me”

When we say, ٱلرَّحْمَـٰنِ ٱلرَّحِيمِ

Allah says, “My Slave has Praised Me more”

When we say, مَـٰلِكِ يَوْمِ ٱلدِّينِ

Allah says, “My Slave has glorified Me”

When we say, إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ

Allah says, “This is between Me and My Slave and My Slave shall have whatever he or she is asking for

When we say, ٱهْدِنَا ٱلصِّرَٰطَ ٱلْمُسْتَقِيمَ

Allah says, “It is guaranteed”

When we say, صِرَٰطَ ٱلَّذِينَ أَنْعَمْتَ عَلَيْهِمْ

Allah says, “It is guaranteed”

When we say, غَيْرِ ٱلْمَغْضُوبِ عَلَيْهِمْ وَلَا ٱلضَّآلِّينَ

Allah says, “It is guaranteed”
