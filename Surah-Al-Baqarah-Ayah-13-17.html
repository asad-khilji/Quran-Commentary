Surah Al Baqarah: Ayah 13-17

By: Nouman Ali Khan

وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمْ ءَامِنُوا۟ كَمَآ ءَامَنَ ٱلنَّاسُ قَالُوٓا۟ أَنُؤْمِنُ كَمَآ ءَامَنَ ٱلسُّفَهَآءُ ۗ أَلَآ إِنَّهُمْ هُمُ ٱلسُّفَهَآءُ وَلَـٰكِن لَّا يَعْلَمُونَ

  • And when it is said to them believe just as the people have
  • This is positive reinforcement and encouragement for everyone
  • But the response of the hypocrites gets worse
  • They say should we believe as the fools have
  • They call those who made sacrifices stupid
  • In Makkah it was harder to accept Islam and they went to sacrifices
  • In Madinah it was easier to accept Islam and their faith was not tested
  • The people of Makkah had to leave their homes
  • The hypocrites said you want us to be humiliated like them
  • Listen up! They are the ones who are the fools and they know not
  • Allah comes to the defense of the sahaba
  • The greatest example of people who believe are the sahaba
  • Offending the sahaba is an offence to Allah

وَإِذَا لَقُوا۟ ٱلَّذِينَ ءَامَنُوا۟ قَالُوٓا۟ ءَامَنَّا وَإِذَا خَلَوْا۟ إِلَىٰ شَيَـٰطِينِهِمْ قَالُوٓا۟ إِنَّا مَعَكُمْ إِنَّمَا نَحْنُ مُسْتَهْزِءُونَ

  • And when they are with the believers they say we are with you
  • But when they are alone with their devils they say we are with you
  • We were only mocking them
  • In private the hypocrites are making fun of the believers
  • In public they say they are with the believers
  • The devils are the Jews and Rabbis who conspired with them
  • Shaytan means to be overcome with jealousy
  • Shaytan means someone to plans and schemes in private

ٱللَّهُ يَسْتَهْزِئُ بِهِمْ وَيَمُدُّهُمْ فِى طُغْيَـٰنِهِمْ يَعْمَهُونَ

  • Allah in fact is the one who is mocking them
  • He extends them in their rebellion
  • Allah allows them more and more opportunities to rebel
  • It is like a long leash
  • With a 1 foot leash the dog does not run too far
  • With a 1000 ft leash the dog runs very fast then its choked to death
  • The hypocrites are digging their own hole in hell and in the grave
  • Anyone that crosses a line because they can out of arrogance
  • They are blind to the reality of their hypocrisy
  • They are in doubt with no clarity in direction

أُو۟لَـٰٓئِكَ ٱلَّذِينَ ٱشْتَرَوُا۟ ٱلضَّلَـٰلَةَ بِٱلْهُدَىٰ فَمَا رَبِحَت تِّجَـٰرَتُهُمْ وَمَا كَانُوا۟ مُهْتَدِينَ

  • Those are the people who have traded guidance for misguidance
  • Humans prefer cash as a salesman because the rewards are immediate
  • Allah offers Jannah on a credit deal and we just have His word
  • There is a level of trust involved in a credit deal
  • Shaytan offers duniya on a cash deal and it is immediate
  • The hypocrites took a cash deal from shaytan
  • Their transaction had no benefit and it did not materialize at all
  • Their deal did not go through at all
  • They were never committed to guidance at all
  • The hypocrites pretend to be committed to guidance

مَثَلُهُمْ كَمَثَلِ ٱلَّذِى ٱسْتَوْقَدَ نَارًۭا فَلَمَّآ أَضَآءَتْ مَا حَوْلَهُۥ ذَهَبَ ٱللَّهُ بِنُورِهِمْ وَتَرَكَهُمْ فِى ظُلُمَـٰتٍۢ لَّا يُبْصِرُونَ

  • Their example is like the example of someone who tried to light a fire
  • This is the example of the Messenger giving them the light of Quran
  • Then when it lit up what was around them Allah took away their light
  • The light of Quran is around them but they lack the ability to see it as it is
  • What good is a fire when you cannot see
  • Revelation came and they did not appreciate the revelation
  • Then Allah took away that ability to accept guidance
  • Accepting guidance is a muscle that must be exercised
  • The Hypocrites did not exercise their ability to accept the guidance
  • And He left them in multiple layers of darkness
  • They have so many problems of this world
  • No more insight left for them
