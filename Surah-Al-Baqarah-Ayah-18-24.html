Surah Al Baqarah: Ayah 18-24

By: Nouman Ali Khan

صُمٌّۢ بُكْمٌ عُمْىٌۭ فَهُمْ لَا يَرْجِعُونَ

  • They are Deaf, they cannot hear, and they cannot be heard
  • They are Mute and they cannot speak
  • They are blind, they cannot see, and they cannot be seen
  • Their ability to hear the guidance is taken away
  • Their ability to see the character of the Prophet or Muslims is taken away
  • Their ability to ask for the guidance is taken away
  • They have been abandoned
  • They are not going to return to the guidance or the fire that was lit
  • They will not be returning back to the Messenger to be guided

أَوْ كَصَيِّبٍۢ مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ فِيهِ ظُلُمَـٰتٌۭ وَرَعْدٌۭ وَبَرْقٌۭ يَجْعَلُونَ أَصَـٰبِعَهُمْ فِىٓ ءَاذَانِهِم مِّنَ ٱلصَّوَٰعِقِ حَذَرَ ٱلْمَوْتِ ۚ وَٱللَّهُ مُحِيطٌۢ بِٱلْكَـٰفِرِينَ
