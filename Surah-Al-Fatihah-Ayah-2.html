Surah Al Fatihah: Ayah 2

By: Nouman Ali Khan

Rahm

  • Rahm means the womb of the mother
  • It comes from the same root word as the word Rahma
  • In the womb of the mother everything is taken care of
  • The child is completely wrapped up in the care of the mother
  • The child has no idea how much love and care its mother is showing it
  • All burdens and difficulties are taken care of by the mother
  • When a child cries it goes back to its mother

Rahma

  • Rahma means the love and care of Allah
  • Allah is 70 times for loving and caring than our mothers
  • Allah is constantly taking care of us even when we don’t remember Him
  • When we sin we turn back to Allah

The Three Qualities of Ar-Rahman

  1. The Love and Care of Allah is Extreme
  2. The Love and Care of Allah is happening right now
  3. The Love and Care of Allah is temporary and it could be take away

The Two Qualities of Ar-Raheem

  1. The Love and Care of Allah is Permanent
  2. The Love and Care of Allah is not happening right now

Example

  • You are really hungry
  • Your wife is making dinner
  • While she is making dinner she says what do you want to eat tomorrow
  • You say I don’t care about tomorrow I need my food right now
  • Then after dinner is served and you eat, you say what about tomorrow
  • We can have this or that

Reality

  • Allah mentions Ar Rahman first then Ar Raheem
  • Allah takes care of our immediate needs first then our future
  • Allah knows who He created
  • We are impatient human beings and we need everything right now

Ibn Abbas

  • Ar Rahman is for everyone in this world
  • Ar Raheem is for the believers in the next world
  • This world is temporary like the qualities of Ar Rahman are temporary
  • The next world is permanent like the qualities of Ar Raheem are permanent

An Ocean with Waves

  • You cannot imagine an ocean with big waves and a calm ocean at the same time
  • The qualities of Ar Rahman are like an ocean with big waves
  • The qualities of Ar Raheem are like a calm ocean
  • Allah is both qualities of Ar Rahman and Ar Raheem at the same time
  • The Rahma of Allah is beyond human comprehension

Concluding Remarks

  • We started with praise and thanks is for Allah
  • Then we said Master of all nations and people
  • The quality of this Master is always extremely loving and caring
  • Now tell me any other Master that is loving and caring like Allah
  • You will not find anyone ever
  • We are not even capable of being loving and caring to our own selves
  • Anything Allah tells us to do is for our benefit only
  • Allah does not need anything and He does not benefit from us serving Him
  • The best of our deeds are only for our selves
  • Human beings put burdens on themselves and Allah removes them
