The 14 Degrees of Love in Arabic

  1. ْالحُب (‘Al Hob): The opposite of hate, the boiling of emotions when meeting the loved one.
  2. الصَّبابةُ: (‘A’ssibaba): Longing to the loved one when they are not present.
  3. الهَوَى: (‘Al Hawa):When you put the loved one’s needs first.
  4. العَلاقَة: (‘Al “Alaaqa) Love which is inseparable from the heart.
  5. الجَوَى: (‘Al Jawa”) Love that can cause illness and distress.
  6. الخُلَّة: (‘Al “Khulla) love that harbours no ill feelings, derived from “Khaleel.
  7. الكِْف: (‘Al Kilf): Love that can distract the heart and overwhelm it.
  8. العِشْق: (‘Al “Ishq): To exceed the limits of love. Often translated as Adulation.
  9. الشَّغَف: (‘Aša”ghaf) Love that overtakes the darkness of the heart. Often translated as Passion.
  10. الشَّعَف: (‘Aša”af): Love that can kill.
  11. التَّيْم: (‘Attaym): Love that can drive the lover crazy.
  12. البَتَل: (‘Al Batal): Love that defeats the lover.
  13. الوَلَه: (‘Al Walah): Love that if lost drives the lover into mindlessness.
  14. الهِيام: (‘Al Hiyam): Love that drives the lover into mindlessness.
