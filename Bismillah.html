The Power of Bismillah

By: Nouman Ali Khan

Ba

  • It is the opening of every surah
  • The opening letter ba is written in length
  • For glorifying and magnifying the power of this letter
  • Whatever you are doing it is attached to Bismillah
  • Before anything you do you say Bismillah
  • Before we eat we say Bismillah
  • Some things are attached to Allah’s name and other things are not
  • When you call on Allah’s name and anything you do afterwards has blessings and barakah
  • The same thing is increased in benefit with Bismillah
  • Everything is incomplete without Bismillah

Ism

  • It comes from a word meaning height
  • When we call Allah we use the word ism
  • We are saying Allah’s name are high and with great status
  • Just saying the names of Allah is a great honor for us
  • Allah tells us to call Him by His most beautiful names
  • When we call Allah with the name Allah
  • Then we call on all the attributes of Allah
  • The word Allah includes all His attributes in one word
  • We don’t know which name of Allah we need
  • So with one name we call on all the names and attributes of Allah
  • Because we do not know what we need
  • Allah knows the nature of the chest, what we know and what we don’t know
  • Out of all the names of Allah, Allah added the names Ar Rahman and Ar Raheem

Allah's Name

  • Allah’s name comes first before anything we do
  • Our tasks become secondary and Allah becomes primary
  • It is a conscience awareness of the dependency to Allah
  • With Allah’s name I eat
  • Nothing will happen had it not been for Allah’s blessed name

Ilah

  • There is no possibility of anyone to be worshipped
  • When someone is desperate and they do not know who to turn to
  • The first alarm in times of desperate
  • The one who we turn to in desperate times
  • To be obsessed with someone
  • Allah removes loneliness from you
  • There are 14 different words for love in Arabic
  • The last one is the one that kills you
  • The one before that is walah
  • Walah is a love that fills you and is enough for you
  • When they are there you are happy and when they are not there you are unhappy
  • Walah comes from the root of Ilah
  • There is no love experienced without Allah
  • Out fear should be overrun with the love of Allah
